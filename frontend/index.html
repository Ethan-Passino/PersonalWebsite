About Me

Welcome to my portfolio! I’m a dedicated software engineer with a passion for creating impactful projects. My expertise spans full-stack development, backend optimization, and crafting user-friendly interfaces. From building robust web applications to exploring cutting-edge technologies, I bring creativity and technical proficiency to every challenge.

My programming journey began in 2016 during a high school computer science class where I first encountered Python. I quickly became fascinated by the potential of coding and created a variety of projects, from console-based tools to simple games. The following year, in AP Computer Science, I expanded my skills with Java and built several games from scratch. Since then, I’ve continued to grow my skills by exploring new languages, frameworks, and technologies, with a constant passion for creating impactful software.
